Advanced Facilities & Libraries

Take a closer look at our high-performance technical research environments.

The Central Digital Library Hub

Our library hub is a modern, climate-controlled facility designed for deep research. Spanning four levels, it provides real-time access to major global scientific databases like IEEE Xplore, ScienceDirect, and ACM Digital Library, along with over 90,000 physical volumes.

Features include individual silent study spaces, group research setups, automated book returns, and high-speed terminals for deep data analysis.

10 Gbps Converged Backbone

High-speed internet access across all lecture rooms, laboratories, and residential student hubs.

Premium Residential Hostels

Fully equipped living quarters with private study areas, gym facilities, and organic dining services.

Venture Incubation Sandbox

A dedicated startup development space with 3D printers, server arrays, and early-stage funding support.
